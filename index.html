The shelf they don't want you to read

Books they tried to ban

BookHoard tracks every book banned from US schools using data from PEN America's Index of School Book Bans — over 6,000 titles documented since 2021.

If a book in your library has been banned, you'll see exactly where, in which states and school districts, and how many times. You can also browse the full list and filter by state in the Discover tab.

Free for all users. No subscription required.
