Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your BookHoard account and all associated data.

Delete from the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the app:

  1. Open BookHoard and go to Profile
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Can't access the app?

If you no longer have access to the app or your device, you can request account deletion by email. Include the email address you used to create your BookHoard account.

✉️ Request Account Deletion

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 3 business days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account credentials and profile information
  • Your entire book library, including ratings, notes, and tags
  • Your Want to Read and custom lists
  • Your reading preferences and recommendation history
  • Any active Hoard+ subscription (you will not be charged further)

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have an active Hoard+ subscription, please cancel it through your device's app store settings to avoid being charged after deletion.
